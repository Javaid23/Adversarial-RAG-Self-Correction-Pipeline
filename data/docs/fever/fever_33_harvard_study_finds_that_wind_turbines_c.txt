[claim] Harvard study finds that wind turbines create MORE global warming than the fossil fuels they eliminate
[candidate_evidence]
- (NOT ENOUGH INFO) Fossil fuel: Carbon dioxide is a greenhouse gas that increases radiative forcing and contributes to global warming along with ocean acidification.
- (NOT ENOUGH INFO) Petroleum: The use of petroleum as fuel causes global warming and ocean acidification.
- (REFUTES) Wind power: Compared with other low carbon power sources, wind turbines have some of the lowest global warming potential per unit of electrical energy generated.
- (NOT ENOUGH INFO) Wind power: Arrays of large turbines, known as wind farms, have become an increasingly important source of renewable energy and are used in many countries as part of a strategy to reduce their reliance on fossil fuels.
- (REFUTES) Wind turbine: Over 1,500 tons of carbon dioxide per year can be eliminated by using a one-megawatt turbine instead of one megawatt of energy from a fossil fuel.
